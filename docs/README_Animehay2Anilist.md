# ☁️ Animehay2Anilist Sync

<img src="../assets/sync.png" width="50%" alt="Animehay2Anilist">

Say goodbye to manual tracking! This clever little script automatically updates your [AniList](https://anilist.co) profile while you watch. 💖

### ✨ Magic Features
- **Auto-Syncs:** Silently logs your progress behind the scenes!
- **Resume Anywhere:** Remembers exactly where you left off, down to the second. ⏱️
- **Works Everywhere:** Busts through iframes to catch your progress accurately.
- **Easy Setup:** Just paste your AniList token once, and you're set! 🔑
